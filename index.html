Blockchain platform for makers & doers

Indium.Network is an open Ethereum sidechain with Proof of Authority consensus, reached by independent validators. We are building a public network for smart contracts that combines speed, security, and cost efficiency.

How it works

1You propose your app idea to the entire Indium community
2The community gives you a token grant linked with milestones
3You claim the tokens by providing a proof-of-progress

Faster & Cheaper

Our proof-of-authority consensus algorithm ensures transaction confirmation within 30 seconds at a fraction of cost compared to Ethereum & Bitcoin.

Reduced regulatory risk

The validator nodes are not anonymous. This helps application, if they choose to, to comply with KYC, AML & other regulations.

Aligned incentives for public goods

Indium.network utilizes ideas like token-curated registries to ensure the long-term health of the ecosystem.
